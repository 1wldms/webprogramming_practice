Motivation

When traveling to or moving to a new region, people often don’t know how to dress appropriately for the local weather. This website aims to reduce the time and stress spent worrying about what to wear by providing helpful, weather-based outfit suggestions.

All three of us have lived abroad and never experienced the full four seasons of Korea. We’ve personally struggled with choosing the right clothes for unfamiliar weather conditions.

Even with basic weather apps showing temperature and humidity, it was difficult to tell how cold or hot it would actually feel. That’s why we created Style-It — to help people like us get smart, sensible outfit suggestions every day.
